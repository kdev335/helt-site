How We Engage

Flexible Formats for Every Need

No two organizations are alike, so we don't force a one-size-fits-all model. Here are the most common ways clients work with us.

Brand Sprint

An intensive, time-boxed engagement — typically one to two weeks — designed to establish or refresh your brand foundations quickly. Ideal for startups, rebrands, or organizations preparing for a major milestone.

Strategy Workshop

A facilitated half-day or full-day session that brings your team together around a specific challenge — positioning, messaging, audience strategy, or launch planning. You leave with a clear action plan.

Ongoing Retainer

For organizations that need a strategic partner month over month. Retainer clients receive priority access, regular check-ins, and a dedicated allocation of consulting hours each month.

FAQ

Common Questions

Answers to the things clients ask us most often.

What types of organizations do you typically work with?
We work primarily with small-to-medium businesses, nonprofits, publishers, and individual authors. Our clients range from early-stage startups looking for their first brand identity to established organizations navigating a rebrand or new market entry. If you have a story to tell and need help telling it well, we're probably a good fit.
How long does a typical branding engagement take?
It depends on scope. A focused brand sprint can be completed in one to two weeks, while a comprehensive brand strategy with messaging, visual direction, and guidelines typically takes four to eight weeks. We'll scope the timeline together during our initial conversation so there are no surprises.
Do you handle design and production, or only strategy?
Our core focus is strategy and consulting, but we regularly collaborate with trusted design, development, and production partners to bring work to life. For many clients we serve as a strategic hub — defining the direction and then coordinating execution with specialist partners or your in-house team.
Can you help with just one part of the process, like a book proposal?
Absolutely. While many clients engage us across multiple phases, we're happy to scope a focused engagement around a single deliverable — whether that's a book proposal, a messaging framework, a campaign plan, or a competitive audit. Whatever you need, we'll tailor the engagement accordingly.
What does pricing look like?
Every engagement is scoped individually based on your goals, timeline, and the depth of work required. We provide a clear proposal and fixed-fee quote after our initial discovery conversation so you know exactly what to expect. Reach out and we'll put together a custom recommendation for you.

Let's build something worth talking about.

Tell us about your project and we'll follow up with a tailored recommendation — no pressure, no jargon.
