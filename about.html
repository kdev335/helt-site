About Us

Built on Stories. Driven by Strategy.

We're a small, intentional consultancy that believes the best brands are the ones built with honesty, clarity, and a deep understanding of who they serve.

Our Mission

Helping Organizations Tell Stories That Matter

Every brand sits on top of a story — about why you started, who you serve, and what change you're trying to create. Our job is to uncover that story, sharpen it, and help you share it with the people who need to hear it most.

What We Believe

Our Values

These aren't posters on the wall — they're how we make decisions every day.

Clarity First

We believe in cutting through noise and jargon. If we can't explain it simply, we haven't understood it well enough.

Partnership Over Prescription

We don't disappear into a back room and hand you a deliverable. We work alongside you, building capacity as we go.

Integrity Always

We'll tell you what you need to hear, not just what you want to hear. Trust is earned through candor and follow-through.

Depth Over Flash

We're more interested in building brands that endure than ones that chase trends. Substance first, style second.

Every Story Counts

Whether it's a Fortune 500 or a first-time author, we treat every project with the same care and creative energy.

Meet the Founder

Heidi L. Toboni

Founder & Principal Consultant

Heidi founded HeLT Consulting with a passion for books and branding. As a brand specialist, she is an advocate for helping thought leaders, authors, nonprofits, and growing businesses present their message and market themselves with the same excellence they bring to their products and services.

Before founding HeLT, Heidi spent years in the publishing and marketing world — advising authors, developing books, and building brand programs for mission-driven organizations. She combines excellence in strategic brand positioning with exceptional, tactical thought leadership and marketing services.

When she's not working with clients, you'll find her exploring Chicago's neighborhoods, reading nonfiction, or mentoring emerging authors and entrepreneurs.
